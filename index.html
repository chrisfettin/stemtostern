From routine wash-downs to professional yacht instruction, we deliver the experience and attention to detail your vessel deserves — throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

01

Boat Maintenance & Yacht Care

The San Francisco Bay environment is tough on boats. Salt spray, sun, and pollution can quickly damage gelcoat, hardware, and systems. Our preventative maintenance services keep your boat protected and operating at its best. Regular care protects your investment and keeps your boat turn-key ready whenever you want to get underway.

Monthly boat wash-downs Professional detailing and waxing Engine room checks and fluid monitoring Vessel inspections and upkeep
02

Hull Cleaning & Underwater Services

Performance starts below the waterline. Our professional dive services help maintain fuel efficiency, speed, and long-term hull protection. Regular underwater maintenance is essential for boats operating in San Francisco Bay's high-growth waters.

Hull cleaning and marine growth removal Zinc (anode) replacement Running gear inspections Propeller and shaft checks
03

Private Yacht Instruction

New to boating? Moving up to a larger vessel? Captain John Curry provides hands-on private instruction for boat owners who want to operate their vessels safely and confidently. Instruction is available for vessels 20'–75' and is tailored to the owner's experience level.

Pre-departure safety checks Docking and maneuvering techniques Engine operation and vessel systems Navigation in San Francisco Bay currents and traffic
04

Captain Services & Vessel Delivery

Need a professional captain? Stem to Stern provides licensed USCG captain services for private owners and yacht brokers. Your vessel will always be operated safely, professionally, and with respect for your investment.

Boat deliveries along the West Coast Local Bay Area vessel transport Owner training and sea trials Professional helm support

Meet the Captain

25 Years on
San Francisco Bay

Make boat ownership easier, safer, and more enjoyable.

For over 25 years, Stem to Stern has helped boat owners maintain, operate, and enjoy their vessels with confidence. Founded by Captain John Curry, the company began as a specialized maintenance service and has grown into a full-service yacht support and maritime consulting business trusted by Bay Area boat owners.

Captain John Curry brings decades of maritime experience to every vessel he works with — from single-screw vessels to large twin-engine yachts, always with a focus on safety, seamanship, and practical boat handling.

U.S. Coast Guard

Eight years as Training Petty Officer aboard the USCG Cutter Hamilton, training crews in boat operations and vessel maintenance under demanding conditions.

Search & Rescue

Qualified Coxswain at Coast Guard Station San Francisco. Over 800 hours underway responding to rescue missions and training operations in the Bay.

Master's License

U.S. Merchant Marine Master's License (100 Gross Tons). Certified instructor at Club Nautique in Alameda.

Vessel Experience

Single-screw vessels to large twin-engine yachts. Experienced across the full range of pleasure craft operating in the San Francisco Bay Area.
